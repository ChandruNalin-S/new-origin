Indian Citizens Residing in India

Individuals
Indian citizens who want to apply for an Individual PAN card must provide valid identity proof, address proof, and date of the birth proof.
Non-Resident Indians (NRIs)
NRIs who generate income in india or are involved in financial transactions within the country are also eligible for an Individual PAN card.
Foreign Nationals
Foreign nationals who wish to conduct business or invest in india can also apply for an Individual PAN card.

Age Requirement

The minimum age for PAN card application is 18 years
