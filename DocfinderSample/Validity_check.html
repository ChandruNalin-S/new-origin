Validity Of The Pan Card Certificate

The PAN car (Permanent Account Number) is valid for lifetime once issued.However, there are certain conditions that can render your PAN card inoperative. Let's explore the details

Checking PAN Card Validity
You can verify whether your PAN card is valid or invalid through the Income Tax e-filing website.

Consequences of an Inoperative PAN Card

  1. Applying for the issue of a credit and debit card.
  2. Paying life insurance premiums exceeding Rs. 50,000 in a financial year.
